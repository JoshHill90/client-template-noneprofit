What is a 501C3

A 501(c)(3) organization, commonly referred to as a "501(c)(3)," is a type of nonprofit organization in the United States that is exempt from federal income tax under section 501(c)(3) of the Internal Revenue Code. These organizations are typically organized and operated for charitable, educational, religious, scientific, literary, or other specific purposes that benefit the public good.

Rocky Mount's Little Raleigh

Rocky Mount, situated in both Edgecombe and Nash counties in North Carolina, is an integral part of the Research Triangle metropolitan area. The city is renowned for its diverse cultural offerings, exemplified by the Imperial Centre for the Arts and Sciences. This venue showcases art galleries, a children's museum, and a planetarium, enriching the community's cultural landscape. Throughout the year, Rocky Mount thrives with various events and festivals, which not only celebrate its rich heritage but also foster community unity and participation. With its blend of history, vibrant culture, and abundant economic opportunities, Rocky Mount stands as a unique and dynamic destination within North Carolina, appealing to both residents and visitors alike.
